Our Story

We believe every business deserves a
support team that never sleeps.

SupportFlow AI was built because we kept watching great businesses lose customers to slow replies, missed messages, and overwhelmed teams — problems that AI can simply solve.

Where It Started

The problem we kept seeing

We worked with dozens of businesses across e-commerce, real estate, agencies, and services. The pattern was the same everywhere: teams spending hours every day answering the same questions over and over.

A clothing store answering “where is my order?” fifty times a day. A real estate agency missing leads because no one was on WhatsApp after 6pm. A digital agency spending half their week on client support instead of the actual work.

“The best support interaction is the one that happens instantly, resolves the issue completely, and doesn’t require your team to lift a finger.”

— The SupportFlow AI founding principle
What We Built

Done-for-you, not do-it-yourself

Most AI tools put the burden on you. Sign up, figure it out, build the flows, train the model, maintain it. For a small business owner, that’s just another job.

We built SupportFlow AI differently: we do the work. You spend 30 minutes on a call with us. We spend 48 hours building your agent. You go live in 7 days without writing a single line of code.

Our Promise

We only win when you win

No annual contracts. No lock-ins. No hidden fees. You pay month to month and you can cancel any time.

We build your demo agent completely free before you pay a cent. If it doesn’t impress you, you walk away. We’d rather earn your business every month than trap you in a contract you regret.

● What We Believe

Our principles.

⚡

Speed is respect

Keeping a customer waiting for a basic answer is a form of disrespect. 30 seconds or less is the standard we hold ourselves to.

🤝

Humans where it matters

AI should handle the routine. Humans should handle the complex, sensitive, and high-stakes. We never try to replace that judgment.

📚

Honest about AI

AI is powerful. It’s also limited. We train agents carefully, monitor them constantly, and tell clients exactly what to expect.

📈

Results over features

Nobody cares how many features the platform has. They care whether tickets dropped and their team got time back.

🔓

No lock-in, ever

We believe you should stay because the product works, not because you signed a contract. Month-to-month, always.

🔍

Full transparency

We share performance data openly. If something isn’t working, we tell you and fix it. No hiding behind dashboards.
